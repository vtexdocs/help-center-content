Before performing the integration, you must create a [Facebook account](https://www.facebook.com/). The integration creates the other necessary data automatically, such as the [catalog](https://www.facebook.com/business/help/890714097648074?id=725943027795860) and the [Facebook Business Manager](https://www.facebook.com/business/tools/business-manager?locale=en_US) account.

The next steps to integrate your VTEX store with FBE are:

- [Installing the Facebook Business Extension and Conversions API app](https://help.vtex.com/en/tracks/integracao-com-o-facebook-business-extension--2hS3ANSZ7vlHCcba4h7k8D/7JvybNGcBXxGKbVWjadKjt)
- [Configuring the integration with FBE in the VTEX Admin](https://help.vtex.com/en/tracks/integration-with-facebook-business-extension--2hS3ANSZ7vlHCcba4h7k8D/jUtgjBDumr5oGWIU7mVLC)
