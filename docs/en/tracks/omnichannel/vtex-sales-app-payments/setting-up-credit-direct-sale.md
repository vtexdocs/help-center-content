If you wish to receive credit card payments on VTEX Sales App, follow the steps below.

1. In the VTEX Admin, go to **Store Settings** > **Settings** > **Payment Conditions**, or type **Settings** in the search bar at the top of the page.
2. Click the `+` button.
3. Click the __Venda Direta Crédito__ payment method.
4. Click the `Status` button to enable this payment condition.
5. You can choose to set up cash or installment payment.
6. Optionally, you can set up special payment conditions by clicking on `Add special condition`. Read the article [Configuring payment special conditions](/en/docs/tutorials/special-conditions) for more information.
7. Click `Save`.

> ℹ️ In addition to the settings above, you must follow the **[Define payment methods displayed in VTEX Sales App](/en/docs/tracks/define-payment-methods-displayed-on-vtex-sales-app)** guide so that the payment options are displayed correctly in the app.
