>ℹ️ This feature is in beta, which means that we are working to improve it. If you wish to migrate your store to the new page,  please contact <a href = "https://support.vtex.com/hc/en/requests">our Support</a>.

To add a product in the VTEX Admin, you need to complete the Product & SKUs forms. Follow the steps below to access the new experience of adding products and SKUs:

1. In the VTEX Admin, go to **Catalog >** **Products & SKUs**.
2. Click `+ Add Product`.
3. Complete the product form fields.
4. Click `Save` or go to the `SKUs` tab if you want to [add SKUs](https://help.vtex.com/en/tutorial/cadastro-de-skus-beta--4ryZ6J45kwn3jDiQBxGiiN#) to this product.

# Product form fields

On this page, you will enter the information required to add a product. See below for more details on the fields you must complete in each form section.

## General information

- **Product name:** Name of the product you are adding. Use simple words, avoiding other languages or complex writing. This field is crucial for SEO and must respect the 150-character limit. Required field.
- **Substitute words:** Synonyms of terms related to the name given to your department or category. For example: "Television" can be replaced by "TV" or "Teevee". This field is essential for a more comprehensive search and must respect the 8,000-character limit.
- **Brand:** Type in or search for the brand name. To do so, the brand must already be listed and active in your store. Required field.
- **Category:** Select the category to which the product belongs. To do so, the category must already be listed and active in your store. Required field.
- **Reference code:** Enter the unique combination of letters, numbers, or both for internal store identification.

## Description

- **Description:** Main information about the product. This should be a clear and simple product description for customers.

## SEO

- **Product URL:** Used to create the product URL. Required field. For more information, see the article [How to define the URL for a product page?](https://help.vtex.com/pt/tutorial/como-a-url-de-produto-e-montada--frequentlyAskedQuestions_368).
- **Page title:** Text that displays in the browser tab and corresponds to the title of the product page. This field is crucial for SEO.
- **Meta description:** Enter a brief description of the category. This field should not exceed 150 characters so that search engines can correctly display the full description on the results pages.

>ℹ️ When completing the SEO information, a content preview will be displayed.

## Operation and logistics

- **Trade policies:** Select the [trade policies](https://help.vtex.com/pt/tutorial/como-funciona-uma-politica-comercial--6Xef8PZiFm40kg2STrMkMV) applicable to the product.
- **Tax code:** Enter the fiscal identification number of the product.

## Visibility

- **Display on website:** Select whether you want the product to be visible on the website.
- **Show when out of stock:** If enabled, the out-of-stock product will be displayed in your store with a [Notify me](https://help.vtex.com/pt/tutorial/configurar-a-opcao-avise-me--2VqVifQuf6Co2KG048Yu6e) field, in which the customer can enter their email to be notified when the product is back in stock.
- **Priority in search order:** Numerical value to set the priority on the search results page. For more details, see [How does the Score field work?](https://help.vtex.com/pt/tutorial/como-funciona-o-campo-score--1BUZC0mBYEEIUgeQYAKcae).

## Category attributes

Depending on the category of the product created, fields may appear for you to complete in the **Category attributes** section. These fields are specifications you can add to your SKUs to give them characteristics.

Here's how to [create your product specifications](https://help.vtex.com/en/tutorial/criando-um-campo-de-produto?locale=en).

If you have created any required product fields, the product can only be activated after this specification has been completed.

>ℹ️ If the product is not visible on your store after creating it, read the article [Why is the product not visible on the website?](https://help.vtex.com/pt/faq/por-que-o-produto-nao-aparece-no-site--frequentlyAskedQuestions_382) to learn how to solve this problem.
