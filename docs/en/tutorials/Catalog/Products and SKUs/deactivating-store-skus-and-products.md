A product is only deactivated when all its SKUs are inactive.

To deactivate the SKU of a product, proceed as follows:

1. In the VTEX Admin, go to __Catalog__, or type __Catalog__ in the search bar at the top of the page.
2. Click on __All products__
3. Search for the product you want in the list.
4. Click on the SKU which you want to deactivate.
5. Deselect the option __Activate the SKU if possible__.
