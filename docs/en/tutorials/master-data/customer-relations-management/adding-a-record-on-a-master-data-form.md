To add a record in a Master Data form, you need to access Master Data and choose the application you want to view. To do this, follow the instructions in the sections below.

Fields can be configured as editable or not directly in the data entity. Learn more in [Data entity](https://help.vtex.com/en/tutorial/entidade-de-dados--tutorials_1265).

> ⚠️ This article covers Master Data v1. It is important to evaluate which version of Master Data meets your needs or is used in your operation. Learn more: <ul> <li> [ Master Data version features ](https://help.vtex.com/en/tutorial/master-data--4otjBnR27u4WUIciQsmkAw#versions-available) </li> <li> [ Master Data v2 ](https://developers.vtex.com/vtex-rest-api/docs/getting-started-1) </li> </ul>

## Adding a record

To save a new record, such as a new customer profile, follow the steps below:

1. In the VTEX Admin, access **Store Settings** > **Storefront** > **Master Data**.
2. In the application, find the form row to which you want to add a record and click the add icon <i class="fas fa-plus-circle"></i>.
3. Complete the form fields.
4. Click `Save`.

## Editing a record

To edit an existing record, such as the information of a customer in your store, follow the steps below:

1. In the VTEX Admin, access **Store Settings** > **Storefront** > **Master Data**.
2. In the application, find the form row with the record you want to edit and click the menu icon <i class="fas fa-bars"></i>.
3. You can search for the record by any field defined in the data entity as searchable.
4. Click the edit icon <i class="fas fa-edit"></i> on the record row you want to edit.
5. Make the desired changes.
6. Click `Save`.
