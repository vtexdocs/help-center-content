At VTEX, it is possible to integrate with the ZIP payment provider. With this connector, your store can make sales through promissories and ZIP system.

To set up ZIP affiliation, follow the steps below:

1. Access the __Admin__ VTEX.
2. Click on __Payments__.
3. Then, click on __Settings__.
4. In the __Gateway affiliations__ tab, click on the `+` button.
5. Click on the __ZIP__ connector.
6. Fill in the __Application Key__ and __Application Token__ fields with data provided by ZIP.
7. Click on `Save`.

## Setting up ZIP payment condition 

1. Access the __Admin__ VTEX.
2. Click on __Payments__.
3. Then, click on __Settings__.
4. In the __Payment Conditions__ tab, click on the `+` button.
5. Choose the payment condition __ZIP__.
6. Activate the condition in the __Status__ field.
7. Click on `Save`.

To set up payment through promissories, access [Setting up payments with Promissory](https://help.vtex.com/en/tutorial/setting-up-payments-with-promissory#).

After following the indicated steps, ZIP connector may take up to 10 minutes to appear at your store's checkout as a payment option.
