At VTEX, it is possible to integrate with the PagueloFacil payment provider. With this connector, your store can make sales through a credit and debit cards,  and other methods of PagueloFacil system.

To configure PagueloFacil affiliation, follow the steps below:

1. Access the __Admin__ VTEX.
2. Click on __Payments__.
3. Then, click on __Settings__.
4. In the __Gateway affiliations__ tab, click on the __+__ button.
5. Click on the __Paguelofacil__ connector.
6. Fill in the __Application Token__, __Application Token__ and __WEB CODE__ fields with data provided by PagueloFacil.
7. Click on __Save__.

To configure the payment methods to be processed by PagueloFacil, access [Configuring Payment Conditions](https://help.vtex.com/en/tutorial/condicoes-de-pagamento).

To set special conditions on payment methods, go to [Configuring payment special conditions](https://help.vtex.com/en/tutorial/conditiones-especiales--tutorials_456?&utm_source=autocomplete).

After following the indicated steps, PagueloFacil connector may take up to 10 minutes to appear at your store's checkout as a payment option.
