At VTEX, it is possible to integrate with the Multipago payment provider. With this connector, your store can make sales through promissories.

To set up your Multipago affiliation, access [Registering gateway affiliations](https://help.vtex.com/en/tutorial/afiliacoes-de-gateway--tutorials_444#).

To set up payment through promissories, access [Setting up payments with Promissory](https://help.vtex.com/en/tutorial/setting-up-payments-with-promissory#).

After following the indicated steps, Multipago connector may take up to 10 minutes to appear at your store's checkout as a payment option.
