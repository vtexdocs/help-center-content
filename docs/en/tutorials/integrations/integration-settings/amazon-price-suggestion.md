The **Price Suggestion** feature in the [Amazon integration] (/en/docs/tracks/configurar-a-integracao-da-amazon) helps sellers track the competitiveness of active SKUs and make more informed pricing decisions.

It allows viewing the current price, the buybox status, and a competitive price reference for each SKU to support adjustments.

## Accessing suggestions

1. In the VTEX Admin, go to **Marketplace > Connections > Price**.
2. Type **suggestions** in the search bar to filter the logs, or filter by **SKU ID**.
3. Click the desired log to view the suggestion details.

When you open a suggestion log, you'll find the following information:

- **Preço atual (Current price):** Current SKU price and a message indicating whether it's winning or losing the Buybox.
- **Frete atual (Current shipping):** Shipping cost considered in the analysis.
- **Preço do ganhador da buybox (Price of the Buybox winner):** Competitor's reference price.
- **Preço competitivo (Competitive price):** Price suggestion to increase competitiveness.

> ℹ️ The **Preço do ganhador da buybox (Price of the Buybox winner)** information is only displayed when you're losing the Buybox.
