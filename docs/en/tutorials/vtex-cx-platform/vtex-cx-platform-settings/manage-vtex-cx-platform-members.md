This article explains how to add, remove, and change member permissions in organizations and projects on VTEX CX Platform.

## Managing organization members

To add new members to an organization, follow the steps below:

1. Go to **Your organizations** in the [dashboard do VTEX CX Platform](https://dash.weni.ai/orgs).
2. Click the three-dot button <i class="fas fa-ellipsis-v" aria-hidden="true"></i> next to the desired organization.
3. Click `Manage members`.
4. Enter the email address of the person you want to add.
5. Select one of the available permissions.
6. Click `Add`.

> ℹ️ The new member's status remains as **Pending** until they accept the invitation. To change a member's permission level, modify the desired type next to the member's name.

To remove a member from an organization, follow the steps below:

1. Go to **Your organizations** in the [dashboard do VTEX CX Platform](https://dash.weni.ai/orgs).
2. Click the three-dot button <i class="fas fa-ellipsis-v" aria-hidden="true"></i> next to the desired organization.
3. Click `Manage members`.
4. Next to the name of the person you want to remove, click `Remove from organization`.
5. Click `Remove member`.


## Managing project members

To add members to a project, follow the steps below:

1. Go to **Your organizations** in the [dashboard do VTEX CX Platform](https://dash.weni.ai/orgs).
2. Click the desired organization.
3. On the organization home page, choose the project to which you want to add a member and click the three-dot button <i class="fas fa-ellipsis-v" aria-hidden="true"></i>.
4. Click `Manage members`.
5. Enter the email address of the person you want to add.
6. Select one of the available permissions.
7. Click `Add`.

> ℹ️ The new member's status remains as **Pending** until they accept the invitation. To change a member's permission level, modify the desired type next to the member's name.

To remove a member from a project, follow the steps below:

1. Go to **Your organizations** in the [dashboard do VTEX CX Platform](https://dash.weni.ai/orgs).
2. Click the desired organization.
3. On the organization home page, choose the project from which you want to remove a member and click the three-dot button <i class="fas fa-ellipsis-v" aria-hidden="true"></i>.
4. Click `Manage members`.
5. Next to the name of the person you want to remove, click `Remove from project`.
6. Click `Remove from project`.
