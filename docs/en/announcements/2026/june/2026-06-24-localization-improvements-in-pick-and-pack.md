Pick and Pack now delivers a more consistent experience for omnichannel operations in bilingual regions, with enhanced language support in the application, Admin, and on printed labels.

The update reduces language inconsistencies throughout the operational workflow and improves the picking experience for operators working in multilingual environments.

## What has changed?

With this update, Pick and Pack users can use the application with a more consistent experience across the interface, Admin, and printed labels.

The localization improvements reduce language differences throughout the operational flow, making it easier to perform picking activities in bilingual omnichannel operations, especially in regions like Canada.

Currently, Pick and Pack supports the following languages:

- Portuguese
- English
- French
- Spanish

Catalog translations depend on the information sent by the retailer to the VTEX platform.

## Why did we make this change?

We developed this improvement to reduce operational frictions caused by language inconsistencies during the fulfillment process.

In bilingual operations, differences between the languages displayed in the application, Admin, and labels can increase the interpretation effort during picking, affecting operational efficiency.

With the new localized experience, operators can work in their preferred language more seamlessly, improving fulfillment execution.

## What needs to be done?

No action is required to use this improvement.

> ℹ️ If your operation uses catalog translations, you need to send the translated content to the VTEX platform.

For more information about Pick and Pack, see the article [VTEX Pick and Pack mobile](https://help.vtex.com/docs/tutorials/vtex-pick-and-pack-mobile).
