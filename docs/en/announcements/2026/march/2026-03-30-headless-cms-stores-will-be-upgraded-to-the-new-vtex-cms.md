Starting **March 30th, 2026**, VTEX will begin automatically upgrading existing [Headless CMS (Legacy)](https://developers.vtex.com/docs/guides/faststore/headless-cms-overview) accounts to the new [VTEX CMS](https://help.vtex.com/en/docs/tutorials/cms-overview) experience.

This upgrade introduces a modern, more powerful content management system designed to improve how teams create, collaborate, and distribute content. Enabling faster workflows, better collaboration, and greater scalability.

## What has changed?

Stores currently using Headless CMS (Legacy) will be progressively upgraded to the new CMS experience, giving merchants access to a more powerful content management experience. As part of this transition:

* **All existing content is preserved:** Content types, sections, and data will be automatically migrated to the new CMS.
* **New CMS experience enabled:** You will gain access to improved content editing, versioning and workflows, and collaboration features.
* **Headless CMS (Legacy) remains accessible:** The previous Headless CMS (Legacy) interface will still be available for reference during the transition period.
* **Enhanced capabilities:** Benefit from features that the new CMS introduces like AI-assisted content generation, branching workflows, multi-language, and improved performance and scalability

This upgrade is designed to be seamless, with no disruption to your storefront or existing content.

## Why did we make this change?

The new CMS has been rebuilt from the ground up to better support modern commerce teams and workflows. With this upgrade, you will benefit from:

* **Improved performance and reliability** through a new architecture optimized for content delivery.
* **Better collaboration** with versioning, branching, and multi-user workflows.
* **More flexible content modeling** with reusable components and content relationships.
* **Scalable infrastructure** that supports growing content needs without added complexity.

These improvements enable your team to create and manage content more efficiently, while delivering faster, more consistent storefront experiences.

## What needs to be done?

No action is required to initiate the upgrade. However, we recommend the following to help you during the transition:

* **Review the documentation to get familiar with the new CMS:**
  * User guide: [CMS overview](https://help.vtex.com/en/docs/tutorials/cms-overview)
  * Developer guide: [Upgrading from Headless CMS (Legacy) (legacy) to CMS](https://developers.vtex.com/docs/guides/upgrading-from-headless-cms-legacy-to-cms-overview)

* **Validate your content after migration:**
  * Access the Admin, go to **Storefront > Content,** and ensure your store content appears as expected.

* **Reach out for support if needed:**
  * If you have any questions or need assistance during the upgrade, our support team is ready to help. Open a ticket with the [VTEX Support](https://help.vtex.com/en/support).
