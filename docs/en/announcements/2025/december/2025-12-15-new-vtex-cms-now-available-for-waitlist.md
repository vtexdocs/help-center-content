The new [VTEX CMS](https://help.vtex.com/docs/tutorials/cms-overview) is now available for Waitlist. Introducing a modern, scalable content management system designed to help merchants create, collaborate, and distribute storefront content more efficiently and collaboratively.

## What has changed?

The new CMS replaces the [Headless CMS (Legacy)](https://developers.vtex.com/docs/guides/faststore/headless-cms-overview) with a significantly improved experience for both business and technical teams. With CMS, merchants can now:

* Create, generate, manage, and publish storefront content faster with the **AI-assisted interface**.
* Evolve content using **advanced versioning, version history, and rollback features**.
* Collaborate using **branch-based workflows**, **multi-user awareness**, previews, and scheduled publishing.
* Deliver localized content across multiple markets with built-in **multi-language management**.
* **Manage media assets** in one place with an enhanced Media Gallery.
* **Benefit from a modern architecture** that improves performance, reliability, and scalability.
* **Improve content publication** for both [FastStore](https://developers.vtex.com/docs/guides/faststore) and [Headless](https://developers.vtex.com/docs/guides/headless-commerce) storefronts.

## Why did we make this change?

We built the new CMS to give merchants more flexibility and control over their storefront content while improving operational efficiency.

Key improvements include:

* **Faster content delivery** through a read-optimized data layer.
* **Greater reliability** with event-driven updates that prevent content loss.
* **Scalable operations** by separating content management from delivery.
* **Better collaboration** with versioning and branching workflows.
* **Reduced complexity** for developers through modular schemas and improved tooling.
* **Enhanced usability** for content editing to optimize time to publish.

These improvements enable teams to move faster, reduce errors, and deliver better shopping experiences.

## What needs to be done?

To get started with the new CMS request a demo or installation by opening a ticket with [VTEX Support](https://help.vtex.com/en/support).
Once enabled, your team can begin creating, managing, and publishing content directly from VTEX Admin by navigating to **Storefront > Content**.
