To disable the login to VTEX Admin through two factor authentication, follow these steps:

1. Login with your user and password;![2FA 1](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/vtexdocs/help-center-content/refs/heads/main/docs/en/faq/Identity/how-to-disable-the-two-factor-authentication_1.png)
2. Fill the field with the code that was sent to your cell phone and click on the button **Configure telefone**;![2FA 2](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/vtexdocs/help-center-content/refs/heads/main/docs/en/faq/Identity/how-to-disable-the-two-factor-authentication_2.png)
3. Click on the button **Remover telefone**.![2FA 3](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/vtexdocs/help-center-content/refs/heads/main/docs/en/faq/Identity/how-to-disable-the-two-factor-authentication_3.png)

With that, the two factor authentication will be disabled for your account and you will be redirected to the Admin.![2FA 4](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/vtexdocs/help-center-content/refs/heads/main/docs/en/faq/Identity/how-to-disable-the-two-factor-authentication_4.png)
